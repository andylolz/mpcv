Writing an effective mail

1. Think of an intriguing subject that will encourage them to open it, and make it clear you're local.

2. Introduce yourself, making it clear you're in their constituency ({{constituency.name}}).

3. Tell them you've been looking at candidate's CVs on Democracy Club. Tell them why you think it's important they upload theirs.

4. Finish by telling them there's a link at the bottom of the email to upload their CV, and say thank you! (we automatically add the link to your email after you press 'send')

5. Sign off with your address, so they can see you're really from their constituency.
